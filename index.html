üåä The Crisis We Face

Every year, approximately 8-12 million metric tons of plastic enter our oceans. This staggering figure represents one garbage truck's worth of plastic dumped into the ocean every single minute. The underwater world, covering 70% of our planet's surface, has become a dumping ground for human waste.

Plastic doesn't disappear‚Äîit persists for centuries, fragmenting into microplastics that infiltrate marine ecosystems, poisoning fish, mammals, and ultimately, our own food chains. üéØ Why We Built AquaVision

Traditional methods of detecting underwater plastic are costly, time-consuming, and often inaccurate. Manual inspection requires divers and specialized equipment, while satellite monitoring cannot detect small items. We recognized an opportunity to leverage artificial intelligence and computer vision to create a scalable, affordable solution.

AquaVision AI was developed as a hackathon project to bridge this critical gap. Our system can analyze underwater images in real-time, identifying 14 different types of plastic waste with 95% accuracy. This enables researchers, environmental organizations, and government agencies to monitor ocean health more effectively and make data-driven conservation decisions.
